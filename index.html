Manufacturing
Stationery Manufacturer · Delhi NCR · 30+ years in business, 2 factories
The Challenge

A 30-year-old business scaling rapidly into OEM and custom orders. The systems and informal processes that had worked well for years were being asked to do more than they were designed for. Visibility into production status, capacity, and order progress beyond the office floor was limited and largely dependent on individual experience rather than structured data.

What Was Done

Full current-state diagnostic across order flow, production, and inventory. End-to-end system requirements designed: job state model, machine and tool scheduling, integration with Busy ERP, escalation rules, and operator role definitions. Vendor identification and evaluation initiated in parallel, with demos structured against the requirements brief.

Diagnostic & Scoping + Systems Architecture Active
D2C · Beauty & Personal Care
Skincare Brand · Pan India · ₹20Cr+ Series A
The Challenge

No unified inventory view across rapidly scaling channels. Monthly stock report built manually. Vendor contracts for a reporting system were about to be signed without a structured data brief to hold the vendor against, just a report wishlist.

What Was Done

Converted a report wishlist into a structured data and scoping brief. Surfaced two issues the client hadn't seen: a broken SKU mapping layer that would corrupt any system built on top of it, and a critical vendor gap — no Tally or Marg integration, making financial reporting impossible with the shortlisted vendor.
